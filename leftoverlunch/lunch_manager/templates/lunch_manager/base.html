How does
Leftovers work?

1

Explore the app

With Leftovers app you can save leftovers from your favourite restaurants, cafes

2

Find your favourite

Instead of throwing away perfectly good food at the end of the day, these restaurants offer whatever they have left as a lunch just for you the day after.

3

Reserve your food

Find what you want to eat near you, reserve it through the app, and turn up at the store within the collection given time.
